Who We Are

The Group

Reya Group operates as a structured real estate and renewables enterprise. The Group engages clients, partners, and institutional counterparties through a defined documentation and compliance framework.

All project engagements are initiated only after internal review of legal status, developer credentials, and applicable regulatory compliance. The Group does not operate as a brokerage, advisory service, or investment platform.

Disclosures

RERA Framework

All real estate projects presented through Reya Group are verified for RERA registration status under the Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016. RERA registration numbers, where available, are included in project documentation.

Prospective buyers and channel partners are advised to independently verify RERA details at the relevant state authority's official website. Reya Group's documentation is informational and does not substitute for independent legal due diligence.

General Disclaimer

All information, visuals, floor plans, specifications, and content provided through Reya Group's materials — including this website — is for informational purposes only. It does not constitute an offer, agreement, invitation to invest, or legal advice of any nature.

All project details, including pricing, availability, specifications, and delivery timelines, are subject to change without notice. Reya Group relies on developer-provided information and cannot independently verify all data accuracy.

No Investment Advice

Reya Group does not operate as an investment advisory entity. No information presented by Reya Group or its representatives should be interpreted as investment advice, return projection, or financial recommendation. All purchase or engagement decisions should be made by the individual based on independent due diligence and professional advice.

Data Handling

Information submitted through Reya Group's registration forms, inquiry forms, or any other communication channel is retained solely for the purpose of processing that inquiry or registration. Reya Group does not sell, share, or transfer personal data to third parties except as required by applicable law or to fulfill the purpose of the original engagement.

Intellectual Property

All brand elements, documentation frameworks, and content systems of Reya Group are proprietary. Project-specific materials — including brochures, floor plans, and specifications — remain the intellectual property of the respective developers and are used with their authorization for representation purposes only.

Governing Law

All agreements, engagements, and disputes arising from Reya Group's operations are governed by the laws of India. Jurisdiction for disputes lies with courts of competent authority at the Group's registered place of business, unless explicitly stated otherwise in a specific agreement.

The Founder's Story

From a Vision of Integrity
to a Legacy of Trust

Vikas Khowala founded Reya Group with one uncompromising belief: that real estate in India deserved better — better documentation, better transparency, and better representation. Having witnessed the gap between what buyers were promised and what they received, he set out to build an institution, not just a brokerage.

Growing up in Secunderabad, Vikas understood the aspirations of the middle class — the dream of owning land, a home, a piece of Hyderabad's growing skyline. He channelled this understanding into Reya Group's core philosophy: serve clients not with commission-driven urgency, but with knowledge, compliance, and genuine care.

Today, Reya Group is one of Hyderabad's trusted real estate advisory groups — RERA-registered, documentation-led, and present across every major growth corridor of the city and beyond.

Our Mission

To make every property transaction in India safer, smarter, and more transparent.

We exist to bridge the trust gap — between developers and buyers, between land and opportunity, between aspiration and ownership.

Our Vision

To be South India's most trusted real estate group by 2030.

Expanding to every major corridor, bringing institutional-grade advisory to every buyer — from first-time purchasers to seasoned investors.

Shamshabad / Airport Zone

Hyderabad's New Airport City is Taking Shape

Status
In Progress

The Telangana government is developing a dedicated Airport City around RGIA (Rajiv Gandhi International Airport), Shamshabad. The plan includes a business district, logistics hub, hotel zone, and 50,000+ housing units within 10 km of the airport. This is backed by HMDA's Master Plan 2031, making land near Shadnagar, Kothur and Chevella extremely high value. Reya Group has ventures along this corridor.

Pharma City — NH-44 (Nagpur Route)

India's Largest Pharma City Near Hyderabad

Status
Under Development

The TSIIC-backed Hyderabad Pharma City near Mucherla (NH-44 Nagpur direction) is being developed across 19,000+ acres. It will house global pharmaceutical companies and generate 1.5 lakh+ jobs. Land prices within 15 km of this zone have already appreciated 40–60% in the last 3 years. Reya Group plots on the Nagpur corridor offer proximity to this mega project.

Ghatkesar / Warangal Highway

Hyderabad IT Expansion Heading East

Status
Expanding

Hyderabad's IT expansion has historically been westward (HITEC City, Gachibowli). However, the Warangal Highway corridor — especially Ghatkesar, Uppal Extension and LB Nagar — is now seeing significant IT office development driven by lower land costs. TSRTC's planned metro extension to Ghatkesar and the proposed IT SEZ are key demand drivers. This corridor has high mid-term appreciation potential.

Mokila / Shankarpally — Bangalore Highway

Hyderabad's Western Luxury Corridor

Status
High Growth

Mokila and Shankarpally on the Bangalore Highway (NH-44) are Hyderabad's fastest-growing luxury residential destinations. The Outer Ring Road has made these areas 30-minute commutes from HITEC City and the Financial District. The proposed TSIIC IT SEZ at Shankarpally and proximity to top international schools has driven significant villa and plot demand. Reya Group has multiple active projects here including IQON West Villas.

Miyapur / Bachupally Corridor

North-West Hyderabad: Affordable to Premium Shift

Status
Maturing

The Miyapur–Bachupally belt has evolved from an affordable housing destination to a mid-premium residential zone. Metro Phase II connectivity, the ISRO Antenna Farm zone, and proximity to JNTU and BHEL township have steadily attracted both end-users and investors. Forest Edge (Bachupally) and The Espino (Miyapur) in Reya Group's portfolio are well-positioned in this maturing corridor.
